The Failed Ascensions AU takes place during Saint's campaign, after he has tried and failed to ascend many many times.
He meets Gourmand, Artificer, and Enot, who share a similar issue, but for different reasons.
Artificer is bound by violence, Enot by attraction, and Gourmand by hunger and love of life.
They do silly things together, like yelling at each other about what regions are called, coping with loss, visiting condemned biomechanical gods, and adventure!
